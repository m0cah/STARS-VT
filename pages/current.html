About the STARS Align Space Training Weekend

- Welcome and introductions

- Gen David Thompson speech (foreign threats, USSF response, importance of space domain)

- “User” experience - joint servicemember to talk about how they used space capabilities to achieve victory in another domain

- Panel of CGO guardians from each of the USSF mission areas to talk about training pipeline, assignments and how to better prepare as college students/cadets.

- Space University - 2 hours of mini-lessons from National Security Space Institute, one for each mission area, cadets choose 3 or 4 they wish to attend.

- Hands-on activities in the afternoon

— EW demonstration

— Drone automation integration with space station

— STARCOM simulators

— Orienteering GLP with simulated comm/PNT outage to demonstrate the importance of space
